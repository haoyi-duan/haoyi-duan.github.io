Hi, I am **Haoyi Duan 段皞一**, an undergraduate student at [Zhejiang University (**ZJU**)](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/) majoring in Computer Science and Technology (**CS**). My research realm is **Recommender Systems**, **NLP**, and **Multimodal Pretraining**. Specifically, I intend to study and design methods to retrieve information better, thus making the deep learning models to produce promising results. Additionally, I also pay attention to the intersection of fields and the launch of the systems in real scenarios. Yet, I'm always on my way looking for things that intrigue me.

I started paying attention to NLP in **Attribute Classification of COVID-19-Related Tweets Based on Natural Language Processing Models** [[Repository](https://github.com/haoyi-duan/nlp4if)], a **Student Research Training Program**, advised by *Prof. [Siliang Tang](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/siliang#0)*. To further my interest, I attended the course *Introduction to Natural Language Processing* and completed group project **VQA Training Based on MindSpore** [[Repository](https://github.com/HoffYoung/nlp-project-vqa)]. 

Moreover, I was fortunate to work with *Researcher. [Jieming Zhu](http://jiemingzhu.github.io/)* at [Huawei Noah's Ark Lab](http://dev3.noahlab.com.hk/) as an intern, meanwhile co-advised by *Prof. [Zhou Zhao](https://person.zju.edu.cn/zhaozhou#)*. I joined the task of using massive amounts of unlabeled data through pre-training and learning high-quality audio representations to improve the effectiveness of audio-related downstream tasks. I also cooperated with the team to implement our research results by using the extracted audio in multi-label music classification, which is now launched in Huawei's business scenario. Also, cooperating with *M.S. Liangcai Su*, we finished our project **Beyond Two-Tower Matching: Learning Sparse Retrievable Interaction Models for Recommendation** [[Preview](https://github.com/haoyi-duan/SparCode)] (*WWW*'2023 Under Review).

I have also done the POI recommendation research **Time as Prompt for A Geography-aware Next Location Recommendation Framework** [[Preview](https://github.com/haoyi-duan/TPG)] (*WWW*'2023 Under Review), collaborating with *Ph.D. Yan Luo*, advised by *Prof. [CHUNG Fu-Lai](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~cskchung/)* at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

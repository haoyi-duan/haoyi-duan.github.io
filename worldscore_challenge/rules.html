Competition Rules

Each team should submit generated videos for evaluation via email to ensure fairness. Two tracks are offered: a small set (300 test examples) track and a full set (3000 test examples) track to encourage broad participation. Participants will be asked to provide essential information (e.g., contact email, model details) when submitting their results. Each team may submit up to 3 times to avoid overfitting, with only the final valid submission used for ranking. Organizers reserve the right to adjust rules in response to unforeseen issues, and any such changes will be communicated promptly.

Participants can contact the organizers at worldscore.challenge@gmail.com for questions or clarification. Additionally, we will maintain a GitHub repository with a FAQ section and welcome issue-based discussions for public queries. All official updates regarding rules, deadlines, or leaderboard changes will be posted on the GitHub repository, the competition website, and Google Group to ensure timely and consistent communication.

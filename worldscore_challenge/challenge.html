Protocol

Following instructions provided in the GitHub documentation, participants are required to submit generated videos to join the competition by sharing the video files with our email worldscore.challenge@gmail.com.

Since the competition includes two metrics, WorldScore-Static and WorldScore-Dynamic, submissions will be ranked separately based on each metric.

The competition repository and leaderboard will be actively maintained throughout the event. To mitigate risks of cheating and overfitting, we will monitor submission patterns and each team will be limited to a maximum of 3 submissions. A dry run of the submission and evaluation pipeline will be conducted prior to the official launch of the competition to ensure the stability and reproducibility of the protocol. Participants are encouraged to join this beta testing phase, which will help identify usability issues and clarify any ambiguities in the documentation.
